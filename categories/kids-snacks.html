Fruit Face Pancakes

15 minutes 2 servings 250 calories

Ingredients:

  • 2 pancakes
  • 1 banana
  • 4 strawberries
  • Blueberries
  • Whipped cream

Instructions:

  1. Make your favorite pancakes
  2. Use banana slices for eyes
  3. Add strawberry halves for cheeks
  4. Create a smile with blueberries
  5. Add whipped cream details

Colorful Veggie Boats

10 mins 4 servings 120 calories
Fun Fact: Eating colorful vegetables provides different vitamins and minerals!

Ingredients:

  • 4 celery stalks
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese
  • Colorful bell peppers
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber slices

Instructions:

  1. Clean and cut celery into 4-inch pieces
  2. Fill with cream cheese
  3. Top with colorful vegetables
  4. Arrange in a fun pattern

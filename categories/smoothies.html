Berry Blast Smoothie

5 minutes 2 servings 180 calories

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add more milk if needed
  4. Pour and enjoy!

Tropical Green Smoothie

5 mins 2 servings 150 calories
Nutrition: Rich in vitamins A, C, K, and iron. Great for energy and digestion!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1 cup mango chunks
  • 1/2 pineapple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. Add spinach and coconut water to blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Add remaining ingredients
  4. Blend until creamy

Pro Tips:

  • Blend greens first for smoother texture
  • Use frozen mango for thickness
  • Add mint leaves for fresh flavor

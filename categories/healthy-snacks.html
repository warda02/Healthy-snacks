Homemade Trail Mix

5 mins 4 servings 150 calories

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almonds
  • 1 cup cashews
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin seeds

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Store in an airtight container
  3. Enjoy as needed!

Easy on Stomach Snacks

Gentle, nutritious snacks that are easy to digest and soothing for your stomach.

Banana Oatmeal Bites

20 minutes 4 servings 120 calories

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts
  • Cinnamon to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mash bananas in a bowl
  2. Mix in oats and honey
  3. Add nuts and cinnamon
  4. Form into small balls
  5. Refrigerate for 1 hour

Rice Crackers with Avocado

5 mins 2 servings 150 calories
Health Benefit: Rice crackers are light and easily digestible, while avocado provides healthy fats and is gentle on the stomach.

Ingredients:

  • 8 plain rice crackers
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Sea salt to taste
  • Lemon juice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mash avocado in a bowl
  2. Add a pinch of salt and lemon juice if using
  3. Spread on rice crackers
  4. Serve immediately
